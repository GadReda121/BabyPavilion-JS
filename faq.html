                                    What is Baby Pavilion?
                                    
                                

                                

                                

                                

                                    
We are a TRICARE breast pump and accessories provider who specializes in helping breastfeeding moms qualify for their breast pump through their insurance. Once you complete the TRICARE Insurance Breast Pump Form and upload your prescription, we will verify your eligibility with your insurance and connect with you within 24 business hours to let you know about your options.
 
                                

                                

                            

                            

                        

                        

                        
                        

                            

                            

                                

                                

                                    
                                        
                                        Why should I get my breast pump through Baby Pavilion?
                                    
                                

                                

                                

                                

                                    
Baby Pavilion is one of the top TRICARE breast pump providers. Our customers choose us because we provide quality products and unmatched services. We offer insight-based solutions and recommendations to our pumping mamas to ensure that they have a less stressful breastfeeding journey. Our process is quite simple and easy:
  • We verify your eligibility with your insurance.
  • We work with your physicians to obtain a prescription (if you don't already have one).
  • We provide you with additional breastfeeding supplies upon your request at NO ADDITIONAL COST.
  • We offer some of the most popular brands (Medela, Spectra, and Kiinde).
  • We offer FREE shipping on all orders.
  • We file claims with your insurance on your behalf, so you don't have to.
     
                                

                                

                            

                            

                        

                        

                        
                        

                            

                            

                                

                                

                                    
                                        
                                        How do I get qualify for a breast pump through TRICARE?
                                    
                                

                                

                                

                                

                                    
To qualify for a breast pump through TRICARE, your primary insurance must be TRICARE. You must be pregnant or breastfeeding and have a valid prescription from a TRICARE authorized physician. Please see this link to learn what is covered under your TRICARE plan.

                                        What is the process to obtain a breast pump?
                                    
                                

                                

                                

                                

                                    
You can get your breast pump in just three easy steps:
                                        

                                        

                                        1) Complete our  TRICARE Insurance Breast Pump Form. We will verify your coverage with TRICARE within 24 business hours.
                                        

                                        

                                        
                                        2) Select your breast pump. We offer some of the top brands like Medela and Spectra. TRICARE also covers breast pump accessories for 36 months after the birth of your child.
                                        

                                        

                                        
                                        3) Let us handle the rest. We will contact your physician, if necessary, and obtain a prescription for you. Once your eligibility has been verified, we will ship out your order, and file a claim with your insurance for you!
 
                                

                                

                            

                            

                        

                        

                        
                        

                            

                            

                                

                                

                                    
                                        
                                    When can I order my breast pump?
                                    
                                

                                

                                

                                

                                    
You can place your order anytime, but the best time to order your breast pump is when you have started your 3rd Trimester.

                                

                                

                            

                            

                        

                        

                        
                        

                            

                            

                                

                                

                                    
                                        
                                        How long will it take to receive my breast pump after I qualify?
                                    
                                

                                

                                

                                

                                    
You will be contacted within 24 business hours of placing your order. If all the information we received is correct and you are eligible, your order will be shipped out with priority mail. If more information is required, then a customer care specialist at Baby Pavilion will work closely with you to make sure your order is processed as quickly as possible.

                                

                                

                            

                            

                        

                        

                        
                        

                            

                            

                                

                                

                                    
                                        
                                        Do I need a prescription?
                                    
                                

                                

                                

                                

                                    
Yes! Baby Pavilion requires a prescription by a TRICARE authorized physician. If you do not have one, then we will work with you and your doctor to obtain a valid prescription for your breast pump. 
If you already have a prescription, please send it to us via email at pumpit@babypavilion or fax at (888) 786-5565. Once we receive your prescription, we will review it to verify if it meets the requirements outlined by your insurance.

                                

                                

                            

                            

                        

                        

                        
                        

                            

                            

                                

                                

                                    
                                        
                                        Does TRICARE cover additional breast pump supplies?
                                    
                                

                                

                                

                                

                                    
TRICARE does cover additional breast pump supplies for up to 36 months post-birth. For more information on what supplies are covered please refer to this link.

                                

                                

                            

                            

                        

                        

                        
                        

                            

                            

                                

                                

                                    
                                        
                                        How can I check the status of my order?
                                    
                                

                                

                                

                                

                                    
A customer care specialist will be in touch with you via email. You can always call us at 1-800-616-7860 or email us at pumpit@babypavilion.com. Once your order ships, you will receive an email with tracking information.

                                

                                

                            

                            

                        

                        

                        
                        

                            

                            

                                

                                

                                    
                                        
                                        What happens if my breast pump stops working?
                                    
                                

                                

                                

                                

                                    
Every breast pump is covered with a manufacturer warranty. In the event that your pump stops working, please contact the manufacturer and they will guide you through the warranty process. If you need any further assistance please contact us via email at pumpit@babypavilion.com
